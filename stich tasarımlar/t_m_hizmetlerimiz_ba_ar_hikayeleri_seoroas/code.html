Protocol Clarifications

How does architectural SEO differ from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO often focuses on "tricking" algorithms with keywords. Our architectural approach focuses on site structure, accessibility, and relevance. We build a technical foundation that remains robust regardless of algorithm updates.

Why is tracking implementation mandatory for your services?

Measurement is the compass of growth. Without precise tracking (Server-Side, GTM), we cannot determine which channels are actually driving revenue. We do not work in the dark.

Do you specialize in WordPress or Shopify?

Both. Each platform has unique structural limitations. Our WordPress service focuses on performance and core security, while our Shopify service optimizes Liquid code and app-induced bloat for maximum organic reach.

Can SEO and Google Ads work together?

Absolutely. We use paid search data to identify high-converting keywords quickly, which then informs our long-term organic content strategy. This creates a feedback loop of efficiency.
