What is GEO?
The Paradigm Shift.

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the technical discipline of optimizing brand data for ingestion and synthesis by Large Language Models like GPT-4, Gemini, and Claude.

Unlike traditional SEO, which focuses on link equity and keywords, GEO focuses on Information Density and Knowledge Graph Association. AI engines don't just "rank" links—they synthesize answers and cite authoritative sources.

Technical Insights.

How does GEO differ from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for a list of blue links based on keywords. GEO optimizes for the generative process itself. This requires a focus on semantic completeness, entity clarity, and establishing verifiable "facts" that LLMs can trust and reproduce.

Will SGE replace organic traffic?

It won't replace it, but it will transform it. Users are looking for answers, not links. If your brand provides the answer and is cited as the authority, you win the highest intent traffic possible. We focus on capturing the citation slot.

What is a 'Knowledge Graph Injection'?

This is our proprietary process of ensuring your brand's unique data points are connected to established nodes in the global knowledge graph (like Wikidata, schema.org, and industry-specific databases) used by AI training sets.
